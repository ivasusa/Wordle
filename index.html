Wordle

How to Play

Guess the secret 5 letter word in up to 6 attempts. Each guess must be a valid English word.

After each guess, the tiles change color to show how close you are:

  • Green is correct letter in the correct position
  • Yellow is correct letter in the wrong position
  • Gray is letter not in the word
